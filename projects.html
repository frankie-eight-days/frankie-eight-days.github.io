MUN SAE Aerospace Design Club

Electrical Design Lead

Nov. 2016 - Present

The SAE Aerospace competition is an international event where universities design and build UAVs. There are several tasks that these aircraft must complete. I oversee the team's electrical division and lead them in developing software and hardware. Tasks include:

  • Designing/Fabricating power distribution circuits for airborne and ground based systems
  • Creating Arduino based aircraft information systems using various sensors and RF transmitters
  • Programming software to assist with payload dropping and flight-stability
  • Soliciting local businesses for club sponsorship
